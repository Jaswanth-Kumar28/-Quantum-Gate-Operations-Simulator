Qubit state
|ψ⟩ = α|0⟩ + β|1⟩
α, β are complex amplitudes
Born rule (probability)
P(0) = |α|², P(1) = |β|²
Total probability = 1
Gate transformation
|ψ'⟩ = U|ψ⟩
U is a 2×2 unitary matrix
Normalization
|α|² + |β|² = 1
Always satisfied after gate
